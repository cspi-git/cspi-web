Privacy Policy

Website

CSPI is responsible for all personal information under its control. Our team is accountable for compliance with these privacy and security principles.

We will do our best to respond to any questions or concerns you may have about the privacy of your personal information or about us.

What information is retained when I stop using one of your service?

Depending on the service you have used, you can freely request the deletion of all your data relating to the service you have used.
