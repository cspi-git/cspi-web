Vision

We envision a world where people have secure control over their digital information, while preserving their anonymity and freedom in the digital world.

Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive privacy-focused and secure digital services for everyone. Privacy and security are at the heart of everything we do.

Core Principles

Honesty, Autonomy, Privacy, Anonymity, Freedom, Integrity, Security (HAPAFIS).

  • Honesty: We are honest and transparent about what we offer and how we operate.
  • Autonomy: We believe that users information should not be sold, shared or collected without their permission or knowledge.
  • Privacy: We are committed to protecting your privacy and only collect information that is necessary for our services.
  • Anonymity: We ensure that any data collected is anonymized wherever possible.
  • Freedom: We envision an internet where democracy reigns and all data receives equal respect.
  • Integrity: We strongly uphold our core principles no matter what,
  • Security: As well as protecting your privacy, we make sure you are completely secure.
