Partnership

Partnering with us means we will work together with common goals and beliefs, which are Protecting Privacy, Expanding & Creating Security Tools for Protection, Creating Services that Protects It's Users Security & Privacy and we must or can share specific resources to expand each other's capabilities for projects and hoping for a long lasting trust relationship between any organization or server.

Requirements

  • Your group must the same belief of CSPI.
  • You agree to share specific resources that are required for collaborated projects or works.
  • Your group must care about the security & privacy of It's users.
  • You must have at least more than 100 members in your Discord server.
  • Active server or group.

Addition

The requirements can be excluded depending in the situation.
