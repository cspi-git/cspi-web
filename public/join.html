Becoming a member of CSPI Sec

Hey there, tech enthusiasts! Are you into security and privacy? Want to kick some bad actors' butts and make the online world a better place? CSPI is looking for cool folks like you to join our crew!

Is there a prize for joining?

Nah, we're not in it for the trophies. We genuinely believe that "security and privacy are not a feature, but essential." By joining CSPI, you'll be part of the team making the internet a little safer. Plus, you'll get to level up your tech skills in real-life scenarios!

How can I join? Do I need to be an expert?

Nope, no expertise required! We'll be there to guide you and help you along the way. We just need someone who gets tech stuff and can handle tasks like a boss. You don't have to do tasks if you don't want to—just as long as you share our beliefs and stay within our boundaries, you're good. It's all voluntary, and we respect your choice.

If you're pumped to join CSPI and be part of our crew, please join our Discord server and make a ticket on Information Inquiry.
